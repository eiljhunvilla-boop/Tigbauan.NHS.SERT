SCHOOL EMERGENCY RESPONSE TEAM

PREPAREDNESS • SAFETY • SERVICE

To promote safety and preparedness in our school by training student responders, leading emergency drills, raising awareness about disasters, and supporting environmental activities that help reduce risks and protect our community.

The School Emergency Response Team (SERT) is a dedicated group committed to promoting safety, preparedness, and well-being within both the school and the wider community. SERT trains its members in emergency response, first aid, disaster management, and crisis intervention, ensuring readiness to assist not only students and staff but also community members during emergencies. Through proactive planning, education, and rapid response, SERT plays a vital role in protecting lives and fostering a culture of safety beyond the school grounds.

A well-prepared and resilient school community where students are empowered, trained, and united to respond to emergencies, reduce risks, and promote a culture of safety and responsibility.
